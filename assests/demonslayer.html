Characters of Demon Slayer:

Tanjiro Kamado:

(竈門かまど 炭たん治じ郎ろう Kamado Tanjirō?) is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.
He is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps, who joined to find a remedy to turn his sister, Nezuko Kamado,
back into a human.
Before he became a Demon Slayer, his family was slaughtered by the Demon King, Muzan Kibutsuji,
while his younger sister, Nezuko, was turned into a Demon.
